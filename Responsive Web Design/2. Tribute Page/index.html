Lucho Bermúdez

The man who gave Colombia its musical identity in the 20th century

Some important events in Lucho's life:

  • Luis Eduardo Bermúdez Acosta better known as Lucho Bermúde born in Carmen de Bolívar, Bolívar, Colombia (January 25, 1912)
  • He is considered one of the most important performers and composers of Colombian music in the 20th century
  • He made musical styles such as cumbia and porro symbols of national identity for the Colombian people
  • In addition to his native country, Colombia, his musical career extends throughout all Latin America, especially in countries such as Mexico, Cuba and Argentina.
  • Lucho Bermúdez composed around 1000 compositions, of which the most recognized are "Colombia Tierra Querida", "Carmen de Bolívar", "Salsipuedes", "San Fernando", "Tolú", among others.
